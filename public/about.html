About MyHerbs



MyHerbs goal as an application is to bring awareness to the potential psychological benefits to taking different strains of marijuana. Different strains have different effects because of their unique terpene profiles. Based on those profiles and coupled with the specific benefits that each terpene has, MyHerbs can match users with a strain that can best serve their personal and unique needs. MyHerbs is created and owned by Brian Guan, an engineering student at UCLA.
